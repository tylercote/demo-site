Who We Are

The Mason Small Business Development Center, at George Mason University-Mason Enterprise Center, has been assisting the small business community in Northern Virginia for over four decades.

Since its inception in 1987, the Mason SBDC has served more than 6,000 clients and played a pivotal role in fueling the local economy and business climate. The program has done so, by providing tried and true guidance, invaluable 1-on-1 counseling, training. Every business participating in the program receives thorough counseling support for any stage of its life-cycle.

Thanks to the generosity of our sponsors: George Mason University, The Virginia SBDC Network, and the U.S. Small Business Administration, our group and one-on-one counseling services to small businesses in Fairfax, Arlington, and Prince William counties, are offered at NO cost.
